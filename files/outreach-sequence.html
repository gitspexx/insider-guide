Phase 01 · Email
✉️
Email strategy
Three emails, spaced out. First email has no link — this is intentional. It lowers the spam score and creates curiosity. The link only appears in email 2 once they've shown intent by opening or replying. Email 3 is a clean, no-pressure close. If all three get no response, the channel is done — move to WhatsApp.
01
Email The Cold Open
Day 1 No link · No pitch
Subject Quick question about [Business name]
Hi [Name], I'm Alex — travel content creator, currently building a curated guide to Colombia for a growing community of travelers planning their trip. I came across [Business name] and it's exactly the kind of place the guide needs more of. Would you be open to being included? — Alex @alexspexx
Why no link? Cold emails with links hit spam harder and look like mass outreach. This reads like a human wrote it in 60 seconds. The goal of email 1 is a single "yes" or "tell me more" — nothing else. Keep it under 60 words.
02
Email The Follow-Up
Day 4 Now add the link
Subject Re: Quick question about [Business name]
Hey [Name], Following up in case this got buried. I'm building a travel guide to Colombia — curated spots shared with a community of travelers who are actively planning their trips. The guide lives online and grows over time. There's a small Featured placement that puts [Business name] at the top of the [category] section for $200, or a simple listing from $50 if you just want to be in the directory. Here's where the guide is heading: [link] Worth 5 minutes? — Alex
This is the first time you mention price. Drop both tiers ($50 and $200) so they have an easy entry point. The $50 mention lowers resistance — it's an easy yes. If they bite on $50, you have a customer. Upgrade conversations happen later. Reply in the same thread — keeps the subject line familiar.
03
Email The Soft Close
Day 9 Last email · No guilt
Subject Closing the loop — [Business name]
Hi [Name], Last message from me on this — I know inboxes get full. The Colombia guide is almost ready to launch. I'm keeping a small number of spots open for the [category] section and wanted to give [Business name] a chance before it goes live. If the timing isn't right, no problem at all — I can add you to the free directory and reach out again when I update the guide. Either way: [link] — Alex
Never close with pressure. The "free directory" fallback gives them a no-cost exit that still keeps them in your world. Many businesses say yes to the free listing here and convert to paid later. This email also reactivates people who missed the first two.
Phase 02 · WhatsApp
💬
WhatsApp strategy — handle with care
WhatsApp has the highest conversion rate but the highest risk. Never blast. One message at a time. Space them out. No links in the first message — WhatsApp flags link-first cold messages fast. Maximum 2 touchpoints on this channel before you stop. If they mark you as spam, your number gets restricted. Treat every message like you're texting a real person, because that's exactly what they'll feel.
04
WhatsApp First Contact
Day 14 No link · Very short
Hola [Name] 👋 Soy Alex, creador de contenido de viajes. Estoy armando una guía de Colombia y quería ver si [Business name] estaría interesado en aparecer. ¿Es este un buen número para hablar?
Hey [Name] 👋 Alex here — travel content creator. Building a Colombia guide and wanted to see if [Business name] would be interested in being featured. Is this a good number to reach you?
Ask if it's the right number. This does two things: it feels respectful (not a blast), and it filters out wrong numbers fast. Send in Spanish by default for Colombian businesses. Only switch to English if they reply in English. No link, no price, no pitch — just a door knock.
05
WhatsApp Soft Nudge
Day 20 Only if message was seen
Hola de nuevo [Name] — solo quería asegurarme de que viste mi mensaje anterior. La guía se lanza pronto, aún hay espacio para [Business name] en la categoría de [categoría]. Aquí más info si te interesa: [link]
Only send this if the first message shows two blue ticks (read). If it's still unread, do not follow up on WhatsApp — move to Instagram instead. This is the only WA message where you include a link, and only because they've already seen your name. After this, if no response, stop WhatsApp entirely. Do not send a third WA message under any circumstances.
Phase 03 · Instagram
📲
Instagram strategy — warmest channel
Instagram is the most natural channel for this kind of outreach — businesses expect creator contact here. The strategy is engage first (like, comment genuinely), then DM. Never DM cold without having touched their content first. The tone is contemporary, casual, peer-to-peer. You're not a brand reaching out — you're a creator who actually liked what they saw.
06
Instagram Pre-Engagement
Day 7–10 Before the DM
[ Like 2–3 of their recent posts — not all at once. Space it over 2–3 days. ] [ Leave one genuine comment on a post that's relevant — something specific, not just "great place!" ] Example comments: → "This view is incredible, which floor is this?" → "The light in this shot is unreal 🔥" → "Been wanting to try this — is this the Laureles location?"
This is not optional. Businesses that get a DM from an account that has never touched their content treat it as spam. One genuine comment changes the context of everything that comes after — you're now a person who noticed them, not a robot doing outreach.
07
Instagram DM The DM
Day 14+ Contemporary · Short
Hey! Just wanted to say [Business name] looks genuinely great — the kind of spot that belongs in a guide. I'm Alex, travel content creator — building a curated Colombia guide for my community. Short version: travelers DM me for recommendations, I send them a curated map. Would love to include you. There's a free spot and some featured options too. Worth a chat?
¡Hola! Quería decir que [Business name] se ve increíble — exactamente el tipo de lugar que necesita una guía seria. Soy Alex, creador de contenido de viajes. Estoy armando una guía curada de Colombia para mi comunidad. La versión corta: los viajeros me escriben pidiendo recomendaciones y yo les mando un mapa. Me encantaría incluirlos. Hay un espacio gratuito y también opciones destacadas. ¿Hablamos?
The IG DM works differently from email. It's shorter, warmer, more like a conversation starter than a pitch. Don't mention price in the first DM. Wait for "tell me more" — then send the tier card or the pitch deck link. If they follow back or react to your stories after this, that's a warm signal. Reply fast.
