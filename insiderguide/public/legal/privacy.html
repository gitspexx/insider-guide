This Privacy Policy explains how BCAX Group LLC ("we", "us", "Insider Guide") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal data when you use insiderguide.co and any related domain (the "Service"). It supplements our Terms of Service, Cookie Policy, Acceptable Use Policy, Listing Removal Policy, and DMCA Policy.

Three classes of users. Insider Guide is a marketplace. (1) Travelers browse curated guides. (2) Creators build and publish guides for travelers. (3) Brand Partners / Listed Businesses appear in guides — sometimes by paid placement, sometimes because a Creator included them based on public information. This Policy describes how we handle data about all three.

Insider Guide is operated by BCAX Group LLC, Wyoming, USA — see Legal Entity.

Categories of Sensitive Data

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (health, biometrics, racial/ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, sexual orientation, precise geolocation) unless you voluntarily provide it in user-generated content. Where collected, processing is based on your explicit consent (Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR).

Children's Data

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact legal@insiderguide.co and we will delete it.

About creators

In addition to the categories above, when you sign up as a Creator we collect:

  • Profile data — name, profile photo, bio, social links, country/territory specialization;
  • Payout data — Stripe Connect account information, tax-residency, W-9 / W-8BEN where applicable;
  • Uploaded content — your business lists (typically from a Google Takeout export or a CSV you compiled), narrative reviews, photos, ratings, organizational metadata.

About listed businesses (Brand Partners and non-paying listings)

For listings that appear in published guides, we collect and display:

  • Business identification — name, address, country, category, contact details, social handles, website;
  • Content surfaces — photos, descriptions, ratings, hours, price band, Creator commentary;
  • Payment data (Brand Partners only) — Stripe billing data when a business pays for a Featured slot.

For non-paying listings, the business is included in a Creator-curated guide based on (a) the Creator's first-hand knowledge or (b) information the Creator has obtained from public sources (the business's own website, Google Maps public profile, other public directories). We rely on the legitimate interest of operating a curated travel directory (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) for non-paying listings, which we balance against the listed business's interests through the Listing Removal Policy. Any business may request removal at any time.

4. Sharing

  • Travelers see only public Creator and Brand-Partner data — never other travelers' identities.
  • Creators can see aggregate engagement metrics for their guides but not raw IPs of travelers.
  • Brand Partners receive aggregate-only impression / click metrics for their Featured placements.
  • Sub-processors — see Sub-processors below.
  • Successors — in any merger, acquisition, sale of assets, financing, or reorganization of BCAX Group LLC, your data may transfer as a regular business asset, with notice where reasonably possible.

We do not sell personal data for monetary consideration. Featured-placement fees from Brand Partners are not "sales" of traveler data — Brand Partners receive aggregate metrics only.

10. Your rights

Your Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland (GDPR / UK-GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data (Art. 16)
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data, also known as the "right to be forgotten" (Art. 17)
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data while a dispute is resolved (Art. 18)
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (Art. 20)
  • Object — object to processing based on our legitimate interests, including direct marketing (Art. 21)
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, where processing is based on consent (Art. 7(3))
  • Lodge a complaint — with your local supervisory authority (a list is available at edpb.europa.eu)

To exercise any of these rights, email legal@insiderguide.co with the subject line DSR Request. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

If you are a California resident (CCPA / CPRA)

You have the right to:

  • Know what categories of personal information we have collected, the sources, the purposes, and any third parties with whom we share it
  • Delete personal information we have collected from you (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (we do not sell your personal information)
  • Limit use of sensitive personal information
  • Non-discrimination — we will not deny service, charge different prices, or provide a different level of service because you exercised a privacy right

We do not knowingly collect personal information from California consumers under 16 years of age.

To exercise these rights, email legal@insiderguide.co or use the "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link in the site footer (where applicable).

If you are in another jurisdiction

We extend the substantive rights above to all users where reasonably possible, regardless of residence. Email legal@insiderguide.co for assistance.
