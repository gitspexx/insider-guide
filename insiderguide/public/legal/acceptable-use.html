This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") is part of the Insider Guide Terms of Service. Capitalized terms have the meaning given in the Terms.

Hard prohibitions — content

  • CSAM or sexually-explicit content involving minors. Reported to NCMEC and law enforcement under 18 U.S.C. §2258A.
  • Sexual content of any kind on traveler-facing surfaces (we are a travel directory).
  • Defamation, false light, fabricated quotes, fabricated statistics, fake news, or false attributions to real people, businesses, or organizations.
  • Fake reviews, astroturfing, brigading, "review bombing", review-trading rings.
  • Pay-for-positive-review schemes, including off-platform agreements between Creators and Brand Partners.
  • Defamation of competitors of any listed business, including coordinated negative-review campaigns.
  • Doxxing of business owners, employees, or other users.
  • Harassment, threats, hate speech, incitement to violence, terrorist content.
  • Pirated photos, music, or video in any listing or creator content.
  • Stolen content copied from another Creator on Insider Guide or from another platform.
  • Content obtained by violating a third-party platform's ToS — most importantly, scraped Google Maps lists. Google Takeout of your own saved lists is permitted; scraping public Google Maps results is not.
  • Content promoting illegal businesses in the destination jurisdiction (unlicensed operations, illegal drug venues, traffic in protected wildlife / cultural property).

Hard prohibitions — actions

  • Scraping, crawling, systematic extraction of Insider Guide content.
  • Building a derivative product or competing service on Insider Guide content.
  • Reverse-engineering API behavior.
  • Manipulating ranking, search, or recommendation systems through automated/coordinated activity.
  • Re-identifying anonymized analytics about travelers.
  • Bypassing platform-imposed rate limits or moderation actions.
  • Sharing credentials with people outside your authorized team.
  • Operating multiple Creator accounts to evade quality limits or repeat-infringer tracking.
  • Buying / selling / trading Creator account access.

Sender / publisher integrity

  • Creators must accurately identify themselves on profile (real name or known stage name + at least one verifiable public surface, e.g. Instagram handle).
  • Brand Partners must accurately identify the legal entity behind the listing.
  • All paid relationships must be disclosed under Creator Agreement and Brand Partner Terms.

Sanctions, OFAC, prohibited persons

You represent and warrant that you, your beneficial owners, and any business you list or pay-feature are not:

  • listed on the U.S. OFAC SDN List, EU Consolidated Sanctions List, UK OFSI, or equivalent;
  • located, organized, or resident in Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, the Crimea / Donetsk / Luhansk / Kherson / Zaporizhzhia regions of Ukraine, or any other comprehensively sanctioned jurisdiction.
