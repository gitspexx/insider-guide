Legal hold

Records relevant to actual or anticipated legal proceedings, government investigations, regulatory inquiries, audits, DMCA actions, listing-removal disputes, or platform-policy disputes are preserved beyond standard retention regardless of any deletion request.

End-of-retention process

  • Production deletion within 35 days of next backup cycle
  • Where deletion is not feasible, anonymization
  • Hard copies shredded; digital copies wiped

Your right to early deletion

Email legal@insiderguide.co with subject DSR Request — Deletion. We honor requests except for data subject to legal retention, active engagements, defense of claims, repeat-infringer / suspension audit logs, listing-removal audit logs, or de-identified data. Response within 30 days (GDPR) / 45 days (CCPA).
