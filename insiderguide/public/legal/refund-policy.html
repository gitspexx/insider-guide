This Refund Policy is part of the Insider Guide Terms of Service. It applies to Brand Partner placement fees and any future paid traveler-side feature.

Default rule: All fees are NON-REFUNDABLE

By paying any Featured-placement fee, enhanced-listing fee, traveler-side premium-content fee, or other amount, you acknowledge that all fees are non-refundable upon payment, except as stated below or as required by mandatory consumer-protection law that you have not validly waived.

No impression / click / conversion / revenue guarantee. Featured placements deliver placement, visibility, and visual treatment as described in your purchased plan. Traveler reach depends on factors outside our control (Creator output, season, search algorithms, world events) — variable performance is not a basis for refund.

Limited exceptions

(a) Placement we cannot deliver

If we conclude that we cannot deliver the contracted placement (e.g. we discontinue the slot type), we will refund the unearned portion (pro-rata) within 30 days, less payment-processor fees and any tax already remitted.

(b) Brand Partner — placement not yet started

If you paid in advance and the placement has not yet started and no setup work is complete, you may request a refund within 7 days of payment. Refund net of:

  • payment-processor fees (~2.9% + USD 0.30 in U.S., higher internationally);
  • 15% administrative fee (minimum USD 50);
  • tax already remitted.

(c) EU consumer cooling-off (rarely applies — most Brand Partners are businesses, not consumers)

Where applicable under EU Directive 2011/83/EU and you have not waived your withdrawal right under Art. 16(m), you have 14 days. Activating the placement on or before the end of the withdrawal period waives this right.

Never refundable

  • Fees for past placement periods, even if you didn't actively benefit from them
  • Fees for placements you chose to pause or cancel mid-period
  • Tax already remitted
  • Pass-through fees of any kind
  • Discretionary discounts on a refund calculation
  • Fees paid more than 12 months before the request

Termination by Insider Guide

If we terminate under Section 6 of the Terms or Section 7 of the Brand Partner Terms (AUP / law / quality / fraud), no refund is owed.

How to request

1. Email legal@insiderguide.co with subject Refund Request — [Listing or Account]
2. Include: invoice numbers, amount paid, date paid, services delivered to date, basis for refund
3. We acknowledge within 5 business days, decide within 30 days
4. Approved refunds returned net of processor fees, within 10 business days

Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback without first attempting good-faith resolution is a material breach. We will: (a) defend with full evidence, (b) terminate your account immediately, (c) bill you for chargeback-defense costs, (d) refer to collection.
